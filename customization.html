Custom Products

Whether you need a standard product or a complete custom solution, MCS can assist you. We recognize that special applications sometimes call for unique motor and controller packaging. Our in-house machine shop provides unmatched flexibility and speed in fabricating your custom motor housings and components. We also have over 20 years of experience in customizing our standard motor controller products to fit your electrical and mechanical design constraints.

Over the past 15 years, Motion Control Systems has worked on a number of underwater vehicle applications, requiring high power output and low vibration and noise characteristics. MCS applications engineers can help you pair a low-vibration motor design with an AX servo amplifier custom-packaged to fit your space constraints.

Our engineers work with you to design a partially or fully-housed, complete motor solution that meets your application's needs. MCS has an extensive in-house machine shop and quality control department. Custom motor and controller systems are built in-house and tested together, ensuring they meet your requirements before shipment.
